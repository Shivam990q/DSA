# 🏆 Codeforces Mastery

> *"Codeforces is the gymnasium where competitive programmers are forged."*

---

## I. THE RATING TIERS
| Rating       | Title                    | Color       |
|--------------|--------------------------|-------------|
| < 1200       | Newbie                   | Gray        |
| 1200-1399    | Pupil                    | Green       |
| 1400-1599    | Specialist               | Cyan        |
| 1600-1899    | Expert                   | Blue        |
| 1900-2099    | Candidate Master         | Purple      |
| 2100-2299    | Master                   | Orange      |
| 2300-2399    | International Master     | Orange      |
| 2400-2599    | Grandmaster              | Red         |
| 2600-2999    | International Grandmaster | Red         |
| 3000+        | Legendary Grandmaster    | Red (bold)  |

---

## II. WHAT TO PRACTICE AT EACH TIER
- **Newbie → Pupil**: loops, arrays, strings, sorting, basic greedy, BFS/DFS
- **Pupil → Specialist**: two pointers, basic DP, number theory basics, more graphs
- **Specialist → Expert**: segment tree/BIT, harder DP, advanced graphs, KMP/Z
- **Expert → CM**: tree DP, advanced DP, bitmask, constructive (key!)
- **CM → Master**: DP optimizations, FFT, suffix structures, flows
- **Master+**: research-grade techniques, deep observations

---

## III. THE CLIMB STRATEGY
1. Solve problems ~200 above your rating (the growth edge).
2. Participate in EVERY rated round you can.
3. Upsolve EVERYTHING after each round.
4. Use **codeforces.com/edu** for structured topics.
5. Read editorials of unsolved problems.

---

## IV. THE PROBLEM-DIFFICULTY DIET
- 60% at-or-above your rating (growth)
- 30% at your rating (consolidation)
- 10% below (warmup, confidence)

---

## V. CONTEST PARTICIPATION
- Div 4 / Div 3: for Newbie-Pupil
- Div 2: for Specialist-Expert
- Div 1: for CM+
- Educational rounds: great for everyone (+ hacking practice)

---

## VI. THE PLATEAU-BREAKERS
- Stuck at Pupil? → drill DP + BFS/DFS
- Stuck at Specialist? → segment trees + harder DP
- Stuck at Expert? → constructive + advanced graphs
- Stuck at CM? → DP optimizations + observation-heavy problems

---

## VII. TOOLS
- **CF Predictor** (rating estimate during contest)
- **A2OJ Ladders** (structured progression)
- **CF Edu** (interactive courses)
- **carrot extension** (problem difficulty display)

---

## VIII. THE TIMELINE (realistic)
- Newbie → Specialist: 3-6 months
- Specialist → Expert: 6-12 months
- Expert → CM: 1-2 years
- CM → Master: 2-4 years
- Master → GM: lifetime of dedication

Consistency is the only secret.
