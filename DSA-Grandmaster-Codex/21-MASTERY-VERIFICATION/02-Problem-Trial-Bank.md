## THE TRIAL PROTOCOL
1. Cold start (no warmup on the trial problem).
2. Strict timer.
3. Full edge-case handling.
4. Stress-test before declaring pass.
5. Narrate aloud (for interview-track levels).

---

## SCORING
- Solved correctly in time with edge cases = PASS
- Otherwise: identify the gap (knowledge/recognition/execution/time), drill it, retry.
