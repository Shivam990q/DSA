## HOW TO EARN IT
1. Pass all THREE trials for the level (see [`00-The-Trials.md`](./00-The-Trials.md)).
2. Fill out the certificate.
3. Keep it somewhere visible — a reminder of what you've earned.
4. Move to the next level.

---

## THE PHILOSOPHY OF SELF-CERTIFICATION
External certificates can be gamed. Internal mastery cannot. You know — truly know — whether you can implement, solve, and teach. The certificate is a ritual of acknowledgment, not a credential for others.

Be honest with yourself. A falsely-claimed certificate fools only you.

---

## THE GRANDMASTER'S FINAL CERTIFICATE
When you reach Level 10 — when you've invented, published, and taught — you don't need a certificate. The field itself acknowledges you. Your algorithms run on millions of machines. Your students carry your wisdom forward.

That is the only certificate that ultimately matters.
