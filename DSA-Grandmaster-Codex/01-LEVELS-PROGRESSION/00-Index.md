# 🪜 The 11-Level Mastery Ladder

> *"You don't rise to the level of your ambition. You fall to the level of your training."*

This folder defines the **complete progression** from absolute zero (Level 0) to algorithmic grandmaster (Level 10). Each level has:

- 🎯 **Outcome** — what you will be able to do
- 📚 **Prerequisite** — the previous level
- 🧱 **Curriculum** — exact topics, in order
- 📊 **Problem volume** — how many to solve
- ⏱️ **Estimated time** — realistic timeframes
- ✅ **Exit test** — how you prove mastery
- 📌 **Resources** — books, sites, channels for this level

## 📈 Approximate Time Investment

| Level | Hours (deliberate practice) | Calendar (10h/wk) | Calendar (25h/wk) |
|------:|----------------------------:|-------------------|-------------------|
| 0     | 50                          | 5 weeks           | 2 weeks           |
| 1     | 80                          | 8 weeks           | 3 weeks           |
| 2     | 200                         | 5 months          | 2 months          |
| 3     | 250                         | 6 months          | 2.5 months        |
| 4     | 300                         | 7 months          | 3 months          |
| 5     | 400                         | 10 months         | 4 months          |
| 6     | 600                         | 15 months         | 6 months          |
| 7     | 1000                        | 25 months         | 10 months         |
| 8     | 800                         | 20 months         | 8 months          |
| 9     | 1500                        | 36 months         | 15 months         |
| 10    | ∞                           | ∞                 | ∞                 |

**Total to FAANG (Level 5)**: ~1280 hours ≈ 1 year at 25h/week.  
**Total to Competitive Programming Grandmaster (Level 7)**: ~3000 hours ≈ 2.5 years at 25h/week.  
**Total to Algorithm Inventor (Level 10)**: A lifetime.

## ⚖️ The Skip-Level Rule

You may skip a level **only if** you can pass its **exit test cold**. No exceptions.

If you fail the test → return to that level. The foundation is non-negotiable.
