## 🧱 CURRICULUM (12 Modules, ~400 hours)

### Module 5.1 — The FAANG Pattern Library (60 hours)

There are ~25 patterns that cover 90% of FAANG questions. Master them all.

#### The 25 FAANG Patterns:

1. **Sliding Window** (fixed + variable size)
2. **Two Pointers** (same + opposite direction)
3. **Fast & Slow Pointers** (cycle detection, midpoint)
4. **Merge Intervals**
5. **Cyclic Sort** (1..n problems)
6. **In-Place Reversal of Linked List**
7. **Tree BFS** (level order)
8. **Tree DFS** (path problems)
9. **Two Heaps** (median, scheduling)
10. **Subsets** (backtracking)
11. **Modified Binary Search** (rotated, infinite, monotonic predicate)
12. **Bitwise XOR**
13. **Top K Elements** (heap)
14. **K-way Merge** (heap)
15. **0/1 Knapsack DP**
16. **Unbounded Knapsack DP**
17. **Fibonacci Numbers** (DP)
18. **Palindromic Subsequence** (DP)
19. **Longest Common Substring/Subsequence**
20. **Topological Sort**
21. **Dijkstra / Bellman-Ford** (shortest path)
22. **Union-Find**
23. **Minimum Spanning Tree**
24. **Trie**
25. **Monotonic Stack/Queue**

For each pattern:
- Read theory (1 hour)
- Solve 5-10 problems (3-5 hours)
- Write a personal cheat-sheet (30 min)

**Total**: 25 patterns × ~5 hours = ~125 hours of pattern work. (Spread across modules below.)
