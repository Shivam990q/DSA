# 💼 Interview Training System

> *"The interview rewards the prepared, the calm, and the communicative."*

---

## I. THE THREE INTERVIEW COMPETENCIES

1. **Technical**: solve the problem correctly and optimally
2. **Communication**: think aloud, clarify, narrate
3. **Behavioral**: STAR stories, culture fit

You must train all three. Most candidates only train #1 and wonder why they fail.

---

## II. THE 90-DAY INTERVIEW PREP PLAN

### Days 1-30: Foundation + Patterns
- Solve [NeetCode](https://neetcode.io) 150 (cover all 25 patterns)
- 2-3 problems/day, narrate aloud
- 1 mock/week

### Days 31-60: Depth + Speed
- Company-tagged problems (target company)
- LC Mediums in <25 min, Hards in <45 min
- 3 mocks/week
- Start behavioral story prep (10 stories)

### Days 61-90: Polish + Simulate
- 5 mocks/week
- Full-day onsite simulations (weekend)
- Behavioral rehearsal (record yourself)
- System design (if L5+)

---

## III. THE DAILY INTERVIEW DRILL

```
Warmup (10 min): 1 easy problem, narrated
Main (30 min): 1 medium, timed, narrated aloud (record!)
Review (10 min): watch recording — note silence, hesitation, missed edge cases
Behavioral (10 min): rehearse 1 STAR story aloud
```

---

## IV. NARRATION TRAINING

The #1 differentiator at FAANG is **communication**.

Practice narrating:
1. Restate the problem
2. Ask clarifying questions
3. State examples + edge cases
4. Brute force + complexity
5. Optimize, explaining tradeoffs
6. Code while narrating
7. Test
8. State final complexity + alternatives

Record yourself. Watch for:
- Long silences (think aloud instead)
- Jumping to code without sharing approach
- Not testing
- Not handling edge cases

## VI. THE BEHAVIORAL TRAINING

Prepare 10 STAR stories (see [`../13-INTERVIEW-DSA-UNIVERSE/11-COMPENDIUM-Interview-Mastery.md`](../13-INTERVIEW-DSA-UNIVERSE/11-COMPENDIUM-Interview-Mastery.md)).

Drill:
- Record each story (2-3 min)
- Watch for rambling, vagueness, lack of "I" (vs "we")
- Map stories to company values (Amazon LP, Google, Meta)

---

## VII. HANDLING INTERVIEW ANXIETY

- **Over-prepare**: confidence comes from preparation
- **Simulate pressure**: mocks with strangers (Pramp/interviewing.io)
- **Breathe**: 3 deep breaths before starting
- **Reframe**: it's a conversation, not an interrogation
- **One bad round ≠ failure**: most pass after multiple attempts

---

## VIII. THE INTERVIEW-DAY PROTOCOL

```
Night before: sleep 8h, light review only
Morning: eat, hydrate, 1 warmup problem
30 min before: bathroom, water, deep breaths, quiet space
During: smile, restate, communicate, test
After: thank-you note (optional), note lessons, move on
```
