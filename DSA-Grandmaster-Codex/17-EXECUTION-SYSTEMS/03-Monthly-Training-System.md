# 📅 Monthly Training System

> *"Days build skill. Weeks build habit. Months build identity."*

---

## I. THE MONTHLY CYCLE (4 weeks)

```
Week 1 — FOUNDATION REINFORCEMENT
   Fill gaps in fundamentals you're shaky on.
   30 problems on chosen foundational topic.

Week 2 — FRONTIER EXPANSION
   Learn a NEW topic at the edge of your ability.
   Theory + 20 problems.

Week 3 — CONTEST HEAVY
   4-5 live/virtual contests.
   Aggressive upsolving.

Week 4 — CRYSTALLIZATION
   Review the month's mistakes.
   Re-implement key algorithms from scratch.
   Write a summary blog/note.
```

---

## II. THE MONTHLY THEME

Pick ONE theme per month. Examples:
- **January**: "Master Dynamic Programming"
- **February**: "Master Graph Algorithms"
- **March**: "Speed: solve mediums in <20 min"
- **April**: "Constructive & ad-hoc"
- **May**: "Strings deep dive"

A theme focuses your energy. Scattered practice → scattered skill.

## V. THE 12 MONTHLY THEMES (A FULL YEAR)

| Month | Theme                          | Outcome                              |
|-------|--------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| 1     | Arrays, strings, hashing       | Fluency in fundamentals              |
| 2     | Recursion & backtracking       | Comfort with state-space search      |
| 3     | Sorting & searching            | BS on answer mastery                 |
| 4     | Trees & BST                    | Tree DP intuition                    |
| 5     | Graphs I (BFS/DFS/topo)        | Graph modeling instinct              |
| 6     | Graphs II (shortest path, MST) | Dijkstra/Kruskal fluency             |
| 7     | DP I (1D, 2D, knapsack)        | DP state design                      |
| 8     | DP II (interval, bitmask, tree)| Advanced DP                          |
| 9     | Strings (KMP, Z, hashing)      | Pattern matching mastery             |
| 10    | Math (number theory, combi)    | Modular arithmetic fluency           |
| 11    | Advanced DS (segtree, BIT)     | Range query mastery                  |
| 12    | Integration + contests + mocks | Synthesize everything                |

After year 1: solid Specialist/Expert + interview-ready.

---

## VI. THE MONTHLY DEEP WORK PROJECT

Each month, build ONE thing:
- Implement a data structure library
- Write a tutorial blog on the month's theme
- Solve an entire [CSES](https://cses.fi/problemset/) section
- Complete a contest series (all of [AtCoder DP Contest](https://atcoder.jp/contests/dp))

Tangible artifacts compound your portfolio and cement learning.

---

## VII. THE MONTHLY REST

One weekend per month: **zero practice**. Fully off.

The brain consolidates during rest. Athletes have rest days. So do you.
