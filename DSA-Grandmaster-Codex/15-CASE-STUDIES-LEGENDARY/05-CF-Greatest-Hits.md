# 📖 Codeforces Greatest Hits

> *"Some Codeforces problems become legends — taught, referenced, and loved for years."*

---

## I. WHAT MAKES A CF PROBLEM LEGENDARY
- A beautiful observation that feels inevitable in hindsight
- A clean statement hiding deep structure
- A solution that teaches a transferable technique

---

## II. ARCHETYPE: THE CONSTRUCTIVE GEM
- **Form**: "Construct any X satisfying conditions Y."
- **Approach**: handle small cases, find a pattern, generalize, prove.
- **Lesson**: compute n=1,2,3,4 by hand → spot the construction.
- Constructive problems separate Specialists from Experts.

---

## III. ARCHETYPE: THE "OBSERVE THE INVARIANT"
- **Form**: operations on an array/string; determine reachability or final state.
- **Approach**: find what's preserved (parity, sum, GCD, XOR).
- **Lesson**: the invariant IS the solution.

---

## IV. ARCHETYPE: THE BINARY-SEARCH-ON-ANSWER
- **Form**: "maximize/minimize X such that feasible."
- **Approach**: binary search X; write a feasibility checker.
- **Lesson**: monotonic feasibility → binary search.

---

## V. ARCHETYPE: THE DP-OPTIMIZATION
- **Form**: O(n²) DP that needs to be O(n log n).
- **Approach**: CHT, Knuth, D&C, or Aliens trick.
- **Lesson**: recognize the transition structure → apply the right optimization.

---

## VI. ARCHETYPE: THE GRAPH-IN-DISGUISE
- **Form**: a story that's secretly a graph problem.
- **Approach**: identify vertices/edges, apply BFS/DFS/Dijkstra/flow.
- **Lesson**: model first, then apply known algorithms.

---

## VII. HOW TO STUDY CF LEGENDS
1. Solve Div 1 A-C problems (the "teaching" range).
2. Read editorials of problems you couldn't solve.
3. Follow legendary authors (Mike Mirzayanov, Um_nik, [Errichto](https://www.youtube.com/@Errichto)).
4. Note the OBSERVATION in each — that's the transferable skill.

---

## VIII. THE BLOGS TO READ
- **Errichto's blog** — technique deep-dives
- **adamant's blog** — advanced, beautiful
- **Um_nik's solutions** — terse but instructive
- **rng_58 ([AtCoder](https://atcoder.jp))** — problem-setting wisdom

---

## IX. THE META-LESSON
Codeforces is the world's largest library of beautiful problems. Every legend teaches a transferable pattern. Solve, upsolve, and absorb the observations. Over thousands of problems, you internalize the grandmaster's instinct.
