# 📖 Studying Tourist's Solutions

> *"Gennady 'tourist' Korotkevich is widely regarded as the greatest competitive programmer of all time. His code is a masterclass."*

---

## I. WHO IS TOURIST
- Gennady Korotkevich (Belarus)
- Multiple IOI golds (started winning at age ~11)
- Multiple ICPC World Champion
- Long-reigning [Codeforces](https://codeforces.com) #1 (rating peaked above 3900)
- The benchmark against which all competitive programmers measure.

---

## II. WHAT TO LEARN FROM HIS CODE
### 1. Cleanliness
His solutions are terse but readable. Clear structure, no clutter.

### 2. Speed
He implements complex algorithms with remarkably few bugs. Years of muscle memory.

### 3. Template discipline
A well-honed personal template; reusable, trusted components.

### 4. Observation-first
He often finds the key observation in seconds, then implements fast.

---

## III. HOW TO STUDY HIS SOLUTIONS
1. Pick a problem you've solved (so you know the goal).
2. Find his submission (Codeforces shows submissions).
3. Compare: where is his approach cleaner? faster? more elegant?
4. Note the idioms and techniques.
5. Adopt what fits your style.

---

## IV. THE CAVEAT
- His code is optimized for HIS speed, not for teaching.
- Don't copy blindly; understand WHY.
- His level took 15+ years of daily practice from childhood.
- **Compare yourself only to yesterday-you, never to Tourist.**

---

## V. THE INSPIRATION, NOT THE COMPARISON
Tourist is proof of what sustained deliberate practice produces. He's not a different species — he started as a beginner and practiced obsessively for over a decade.

His existence proves the ceiling is high. Your job is to keep climbing, at your pace.

---
