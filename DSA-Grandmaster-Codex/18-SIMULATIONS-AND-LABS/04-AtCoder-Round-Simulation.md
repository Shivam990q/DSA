# 🎌 AtCoder Round Simulation

> *"AtCoder: cleaner statements, sharper observations, less ambiguity."*

---

## I. THE FORMATS
- **ABC** (Beginner): 100 min, problems A-G/Ex
- **ARC** (Regular): 120 min, harder
- **AGC** (Grand): legendary observation problems

---

## II. SIMULATE AN ABC
- Pick a past ABC (atcoder.jp → past contests → virtual)
- 100-min timer
- Solve A through as far as you can

---

## III. THE TIME PLAN (ABC, 100 min)
```
Min 0-2:   Read A-D quickly
Min 2-15:  A, B, C (usually fast)
Min 15-40: D
Min 40-80: E (the rating mover)
Min 80-100: F if E done; else verify
```

---

## IV. WHY ATCODER IS SPECIAL
- **Clean statements**: less time deciphering, more solving
- **Observation-heavy**: trains the "aha" muscle
- **Strong educational content**: DP Contest (EDPC), ACL Practice Contest
- **Partial feedback**: know immediately if you pass

---

## V. THE EDUCATIONAL DP CONTEST
A must-do: 26 problems (A-Z) covering all foundational DP. Solve all before claiming DP mastery.

---

## VI. POST-ROUND
- Upsolve E/F
- AtCoder editorials are excellent — read after attempting
- Note the observation you missed

---

## VII. RESOURCES
- **atcoder.jp** → virtual contests
- **[AtCoder DP Contest](https://atcoder.jp/contests/dp) (EDPC)**
- **[AtCoder Library Practice Contest](https://atcoder.jp/contests/practice2)**
- **AtCoder Problems** (kenkoooo.com) — track progress, difficulty filtering
