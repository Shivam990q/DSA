# 🎓 CS Core Subjects — The Other Half of Placements

> *"DSA gets you the coding round. Core subjects get you through the technical interview."*

Most placement & service-company interviews test **OS, DBMS, Computer Networks, OOP**, plus **Aptitude** in the written round. FAANG focuses less on these but they still appear. This folder makes you bulletproof.

## THE INTERVIEW REALITY
- You won't be asked to derive theorems — you'll be asked **conceptual + practical** questions:
  - "What's the difference between process and thread?"
  - "Explain normalization. What's 3NF?"
  - "What happens when you type a URL and press enter?" (CN classic)
  - "What are the 4 pillars of OOP? Give examples."
- Prepare **crisp 2-3 sentence answers** with one example each.
