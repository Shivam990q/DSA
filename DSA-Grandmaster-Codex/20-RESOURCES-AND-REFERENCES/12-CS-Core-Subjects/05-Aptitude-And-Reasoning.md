# 🧮 Aptitude & Reasoning — Written Round Prep

> Service companies (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Accenture, Cognizant, Capgemini) and many product companies start with an aptitude/reasoning written round. Don't get filtered here.

---

## THE THREE SECTIONS

### 1. Quantitative Aptitude
- Number systems, HCF/LCM
- Percentages, profit & loss, simple/compound interest
- Ratio & proportion, partnership
- Time & work, pipes & cisterns
- Time, speed & distance, trains, boats & streams
- Averages, ages, mixtures & alligation
- Permutations & combinations, probability
- Mensuration, geometry basics
- Progressions (AP/GP)

### 2. Logical Reasoning
- Series (number, letter, figure)
- Coding-decoding
- Blood relations
- Direction sense
- Seating arrangement (linear, circular)
- Puzzles, syllogisms
- Clocks & calendars
- Data sufficiency
- Analogies, classification

### 3. Verbal Ability (English)
- Reading comprehension
- Sentence correction, grammar
- Synonyms, antonyms, vocabulary
- Para jumbles
- Fill in the blanks
- Error spotting

### (Sometimes) Data Interpretation
- Tables, bar/pie/line charts
- Caselets

## PREP PLAN (2-3 weeks alongside DSA)
```
Week 1: Quant — all arithmetic topics (IndiaBix daily)
Week 2: Reasoning — series, puzzles, arrangements + Quant advanced
Week 3: Verbal + Data Interpretation + company-specific mock tests
```

Take **timed mock tests** — speed matters more than depth in these rounds.

---

## THE STRATEGY
- These rounds are about **speed + accuracy**, not deep math
- Learn **shortcut tricks** (R.S. Aggarwal + YouTube)
- Practice **timed** (e.g., 30 questions in 30 minutes)
- Company-specific patterns matter — use PrepInsta for your target
