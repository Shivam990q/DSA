## A READING ROUTINE
- **Daily (10 min)**: 1 Codeforces blog or [CP-Algorithms](https://cp-algorithms.com) page
- **Weekly**: 1 ByteByteGo issue + 1 engineering blog post
- **Monthly**: 1 deeper piece (research/architecture)

---

## THE PRINCIPLE
Reading is high-value INPUT, but pair it with output (solving, building). A blog read and never applied is entertainment, not education.
