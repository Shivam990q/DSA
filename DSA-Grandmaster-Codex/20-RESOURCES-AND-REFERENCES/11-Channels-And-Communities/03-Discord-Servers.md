## HOW TO FIND CURRENT SERVERS
- Linked from a creator's YouTube/website (verify official)
- disboard.org (Discord server directory) — search "competitive programming", "leetcode"
- Subreddit sidebars (r/leetcode, r/CompetitiveProgramming) often link servers

---

## HOW TO USE DISCORD WELL
1. **Find 1-2 active servers** at your level
2. **Find a study buddy** — single highest-leverage move
3. **Ask specific questions** ("I'm stuck on X because Y") not vague ones
4. **Help others** — teaching cements your own learning
5. **Time-box**: 20-30 min/day, after practice

---

## THE BUDDY SYSTEM (the real value)
The biggest win from Discord: finding ONE peer at your level.
- Solve problems together weekly (voice call + screen share)
- Discuss approaches
- Hold each other accountable
- This can 5× your growth rate

---

## ⚠️ AVOID
- Servers pushing pirated courses (illegal, risky)
- Spending more time chatting than solving
- "Help vampires" who only take, never give
