# 📡 Channels & Communities — Telegram, Discord, YouTube, Blogs

> *"The right channels deliver problems, jobs, and motivation to your pocket daily."*

> ⚠️ **Safety note**: Telegram channels are user-run and change/disappear often. Many "premium course" channels distribute PIRATED paid content — avoid those (legal + malware risk). Prefer official free resources. Search Telegram directly for current legitimate channels; treat any channel asking for money or sharing cracked courses as a red flag.

## THE DISCIPLINE
- Channels are INPUT. Don't let consuming replace doing.
- Max 30 min/day on channels/communities, AFTER your practice.
- Use job-alert channels actively (apply), not passively (scroll).
