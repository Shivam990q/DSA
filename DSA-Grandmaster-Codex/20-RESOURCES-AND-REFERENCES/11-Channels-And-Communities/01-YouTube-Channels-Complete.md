## 🎯 HOW TO USE
1. Pick ONE channel per need (e.g., NeetCode for interviews, William Fiset for graphs).
2. Rule: **1 video → solve 5 problems → next video.**
3. Don't fall into tutorial hell. Watching ≠ learning.
4. Use playlists as structured courses, not random clips.

---

## ⚠️ THE WARNING
The biggest trap is consuming endless videos while solving few problems. Channels are accelerants for DOING, not substitutes for it.
