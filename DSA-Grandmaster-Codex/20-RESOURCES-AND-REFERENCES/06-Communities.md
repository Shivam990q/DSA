# 💬 Communities

> *"A peer accelerates you 5×. A community keeps you accountable forever."*

---

## WHY COMMUNITY MATTERS
- Accountability (harder to quit when others watch)
- Faster learning (discuss problems, get unstuck)
- Motivation (shared journey)
- Exposure (see how others think)

## HOW TO USE COMMUNITIES WELL
- **Give before you take**: answer questions, help others (teaching = learning)
- **Time-box**: max 30 min/day, AFTER your practice session
- **Avoid the drift**: don't let "discussing" replace "doing"
- **Find 1-2 peers** at your level for regular check-ins

---

## THE BUDDY SYSTEM
The single highest-leverage community move: find ONE person at or near your level. Solve problems together weekly. Discuss approaches. Hold each other accountable. This 5×'s your growth.

---

## THE WARNING
Communities can become procrastination ("Discord drift"). Output > input. Solve first, discuss second.
