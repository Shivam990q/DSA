# 📋 Striver Sheets — Complete Guide

> Striver (Raj Vikramaditya) created the most-followed free DSA paths for Indian placements and beyond, hosted on **takeuforward.org**.

---

## THE THREE STRIVER SHEETS

### 1. A2Z DSA Course/Sheet (the complete path)
- **~450+ problems**, organized "step by step" from absolute basics to advanced
- **Steps cover**: basics (C++/Java, patterns), sorting, arrays, binary search, strings, linked lists, recursion, bit manipulation, stacks & queues, sliding window, heaps, greedy, binary trees, BST, graphs, dynamic programming, tries, advanced
- **Best for**: someone starting from zero who wants ONE structured path
- **Each problem**: has a written article + video + practice link ([LeetCode](https://leetcode.com)/GfG/CodingNinjas)

### 2. SDE Sheet (180 problems, ~30 days)
- **~180 hand-picked interview problems** across all topics
- **Day-wise**: ~6 problems/day for 30 days
- **Best for**: focused interview/placement prep when you already know basics
- The classic "crack placements in a month" sheet

### 3. 79/Blind-style + SDE revision sheets
- Shorter revision lists for last-minute prep
- Striver also covers Blind 75 with video solutions

---

## HOW TO USE THE A2Z SHEET

```
Phase 1 (Steps 1-5): Foundations
  - Language basics, patterns, basic math, recursion, sorting
  - ~2-4 weeks

Phase 2 (Steps 6-11): Core DSA
  - Arrays, binary search, strings, linked lists, bit manipulation
  - ~4-6 weeks

Phase 3 (Steps 12-15): Intermediate
  - Stacks/queues, sliding window, heaps, greedy
  - ~3-4 weeks

Phase 4 (Steps 16-18): Trees & Graphs
  - Binary trees, BST, graphs (BFS/DFS/Dijkstra/MST/etc.)
  - ~5-7 weeks

Phase 5 (Steps 17-18): Advanced
  - Dynamic programming, tries, advanced strings/math
  - ~5-8 weeks
```

Total: 4-7 months of consistent daily practice.
