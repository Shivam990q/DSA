# 📋 LeetCode Lists — Complete Guide

> LeetCode is the world's largest interview-problem platform. Its curated lists are gold.

---

## THE OFFICIAL CURATED LISTS

### LeetCode 75 (Study Plan)
- ~75 problems, the official "essential" study plan
- Pattern-organized, beginner-to-intermediate
- Free, with a guided progress tracker

### Top Interview 150
- 150 problems covering the most common interview topics
- The official "interview-ready" curated set

### Top 100 Liked
- Community's 100 most-liked problems (high quality)

### LeetCode Explore Cards
- Topic-wise guided learning (Arrays, Linked List, Binary Tree, etc.)
- Interactive, with explanations

---

## STUDY PLANS (free + premium)
- **Coding Interview Prep** (Data Structures, Algorithms study plans)
- **Dynamic Programming** study plan
- **Graph Theory** study plan
- **SQL** study plan (for data roles)
- **Programming Skills** (for absolute beginners)

---

## LEETCODE PREMIUM (paid) — what you get
- **Company-tagged problems** ⭐ — filter by Google/Meta/Amazon/etc.
- **Frequency sorting** — solve most-asked-first (by recency: last 6 months / 1 year / 2 years)
- **Premium solutions** — official editorials + video
- **Mock interviews** — timed, with company-style problems
- **Interview simulator** + auto-complete-off mode

**Verdict**: worth it for 2-3 months before targeted interviews (company-tagged + frequency is the killer feature).

---

## HOW TO USE LEETCODE EFFECTIVELY

```
1. Free: LeetCode 75 → Top Interview 150 (cover patterns)
2. Premium (when interviewing): your target company's tag, sorted by frequency, last 6 months
3. Track: use the built-in progress + a personal spreadsheet
```
