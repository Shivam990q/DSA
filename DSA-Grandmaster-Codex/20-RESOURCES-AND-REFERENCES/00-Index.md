## 🪔 THE SELECTIVITY PRINCIPLE
You don't need ALL of these. This folder is the COMPLETE map so nothing is missing — but your job is to:
1. Pick ONE primary sheet, ONE primary platform, a few channels
2. Master those before adding more
3. Go deep, not wide

The completeness is here so you never wonder "is there something better?" — now you know everything that exists. Choose, then commit.
