## HOW TO USE GITHUB REPOS
1. **Star** the ones relevant to your goal (build your personal library)
2. **Use solution repos to CHECK your work**, never to copy before attempting
3. **system-design-primer + [ByteByteGo](https://bytebytego.com)** = your free system design course
4. **tech-interview-handbook** = your interview meta-guide
5. **Contribute** — fixing/adding to repos is itself great learning + portfolio

---

## ⚠️ THE WARNING
- Solution repos are for VERIFICATION, not shortcuts. Attempt first.
- Don't hoard repos you never read. Pick a few; use them deeply.
- Verify code correctness — community repos can have bugs.
