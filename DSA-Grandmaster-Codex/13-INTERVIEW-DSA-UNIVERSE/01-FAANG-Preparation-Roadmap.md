# 🗺️ FAANG Preparation Roadmap

> *"A clear plan beats raw effort. Know the path before you walk it."*

---

## I. THE TIMELINE OVERVIEW

| Phase            | Duration   | Focus                                  |
|------------------|-----------|----------------------------------------|
| Foundation       | Months 1-3 | Patterns, [NeetCode](https://neetcode.io) 150, fundamentals   |
| Depth            | Months 4-6 | Hards, company-tagged, mocks           |
| Polish           | Months 7-9 | Behavioral, system design, simulations |
| Apply            | Months 9+  | Interview season                       |

---

## II. PHASE 1: FOUNDATION (Months 1-3)
- Master the [25 patterns](./02-The-25-Patterns.md)
- Solve **NeetCode 150** or **Striver SDE Sheet**
- 2-3 problems/day, narrate aloud
- 1 mock interview/week
- Goal: solve any LC Medium in <30 min

## III. PHASE 2: DEPTH (Months 4-6)
- LC Hards (50+)
- Company-tagged problems (LC Premium, target company, last 6 months)
- 3 mocks/week
- Begin behavioral story prep (10 stories)
- Goal: solve 70% of Hards in <45 min

## IV. PHASE 3: POLISH (Months 7-9)
- 5 mocks/week
- Full-day onsite simulations (weekends)
- System design (if L5+)
- Behavioral rehearsal (recorded)
- Goal: pass 4/5 simulated rounds

## V. PHASE 4: APPLY (Months 9+)
- Apply to 30+ companies in waves
- Maintain practice during interview loops
- Negotiate offers

---

## VI. THE RESOURCE STACK
- **Patterns/problems**: NeetCode 150, Striver SDE Sheet, Blind 75
- **Company-specific**: [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com) Premium (tagged + frequency)
- **Mocks**: [Pramp](https://www.pramp.com) (free), [interviewing.io](https://interviewing.io) (paid)
- **System design**: Alex Xu's books, [ByteByteGo](https://bytebytego.com)
- **Behavioral**: STAR method, company values

---

## VII. THE WEEKLY TEMPLATE
```
Mon-Fri: 2-3 problems/day (mix of pattern practice + company-tagged)
Sat: 1-2 mocks + behavioral prep
Sun: review, system design study, plan
```

---

## VIII. REALISTIC EXPECTATIONS
- Most candidates need 6-12 months of serious prep
- Most fail FAANG interviews multiple times before passing
- Persistence + iteration is the winning strategy
- One rejection ≠ your worth; it's a data point

---

## IX. FAST-TRACK (if experienced, 3 months)
- Skim foundations (Levels 0-4 already known)
- Months 1-2: NeetCode 150 + company-tagged
- Month 3: mocks + behavioral + system design
- Requires existing strong fundamentals
