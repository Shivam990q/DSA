# 🎭 Mock Interview System

> *"Mocks turn theoretical readiness into battle-tested readiness."*

---

## I. WHY MOCKS
You can solve problems alone but freeze when watched. Mocks train:
- Communication under observation
- Time management
- Stress response
- Real-time adaptation to hints

---

## II. MOCK SOURCES
| Source             | Cost   | Notes                              |
|--------------------|--------|------------------------------------|
| [Pramp](https://www.pramp.com)              | Free   | Peer-paired, you also interview    |
| [interviewing.io](https://interviewing.io)    | Paid   | Anonymous FAANG engineers          |
| Friends in industry| Free   | Realistic if they've interviewed   |
| Self-mock          | Free   | Record yourself, review            |
| Karat              | Varies | Some companies use as the actual   |

---

## III. THE SELF-MOCK PROTOCOL
- Pick a random LC Medium
- 25-min timer
- Verbalize aloud, record video
- Solve as if observed
- Review playback: silence, hesitation, missed edge cases, clarity

---

## IV. THE CADENCE
| Phase            | Mocks/week |
|------------------|-----------|
| Early prep       | 1         |
| Mid prep         | 3         |
| 2 weeks before   | 5         |
| Day before       | 0 (rest)  |

---

## V. THE EVALUATION RUBRIC (per mock)
```
Communication:    /5
Problem-solving:  /5
Coding quality:   /5
Edge cases:       /5
Complexity:       /5
Total:            /25  (≥18 = "would hire")
```

---

## VI. WHAT TO LOOK FOR
- Did I restate + clarify?
- Did I share approach before coding?
- Did I narrate?
- Bugs? (target ≤1)
- Did I test before declaring done?
- Time: Medium <30 min, Hard <45 min?

---

## VII. POST-MOCK ITERATION
Each mock surfaces a weakness. Fix it before the next:
- Silence → practice narration
- Missed edge cases → add edge-case checklist habit
- Slow → speed drills
- Communication → record + review

---

## VIII. FULL-DAY SIMULATION
Once a week (late prep): a full 5-round onsite simulation. See [`../18-SIMULATIONS-AND-LABS/01-FAANG-Interview-Simulation.md`](../18-SIMULATIONS-AND-LABS/01-FAANG-Interview-Simulation.md).
